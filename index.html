Upgrade Your Phone Storage

Learn how to expand and free up storage on your device

Ways to Increase Your Phone Storage

  • 1. Use an SD Card: Insert a microSD card and move files/apps.
  • 2. Free Up Space: Delete unnecessary files and clear cache.
  • 3. Use Cloud StorageStore files on Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox.
  • 4. Move Apps to SD Card: Go to Settings > Apps > Move to SD Card.
  • 5. Disable Auto-Downloads: Prevent Whatsapp and Telegram from saving unwanted files/media.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I move apps to my SD Card

Go to Settings > Apps > Select Apps > Move to SD Card?

What is the best SD Card for my phone?

Use a Class 10 or UHS-1 microSD card for better performance
